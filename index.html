Bio

Jake Dulln is a traveling folk guitarist playing roots and original alternatives. Grown up in Maryland,
he now finds the road his home - staying notably in Jackson, WY and southwest Colorado. An astute
follower of Bob Dylan, Jake guides his mind and hands in an effort to deliver something real and unique.
He has played clubs the likes of The Rabbit Hole in WV, Luna Red in CA, and the WildEdge Brewing Collective
in CO. 'Been-a Long Time Since Oklahoma Station' marks his 2nd independent folk album release while work has
begun on a 3rd project. Through trying times, Jake has continued to do what he does, fill spaces with
lasting sounds.
