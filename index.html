Bio

Jackson-based musician (acoustic guitarist/songwriter) playing, one could say, 'Folk rendered through

Welch, Michael Stipe, Richard Linklater, Jack Kerouac, Tweedy, Dave Matthews and Adam Duritz, Kendrick
Lamar, Carl Jung, George S. Patton, Grimes, and onward ..
