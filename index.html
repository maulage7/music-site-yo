Bio

Jake Dulln is a traveling folk guitarist playing roots and original alternatives. Grown up in Maryland,
he now stays in Jackson, WY and southwest Colorado. An astute follower of Bob Dylan, he guides his mind
and hands in an effort to deliver something real and unique. Jake has played clubs the likes of Rabbit
Hole in WV, Mancos Brewing Company in CO, and the Silver Dollar Showroom in WY. "Been-a Long Time
Since Oklahoma Station" marks his 2nd independent folk release while work has begun on a 3rd project.
