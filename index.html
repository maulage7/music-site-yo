Bio

A traveling folk guitarist playing roots and original alternatives.

Raised in a riverside neighborhood on the East Coast and now based in Jackson WY, Jake plays his songs
across the country to Southern California through the bottom lands of Colorado.

Completing his first year on the road, he's played clubs the likes of The Rabbit Hole in West Virginia,
Luna Red in San Luis Obispo, Madonnari festival in Baltimore, and appeared on KSJD in Colorado.

'Been-a Long Time Since Oklahoma Station' marks his 2nd independent folk release.
