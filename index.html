Bio

Jake is from Baltimore, MD and grew up playing music. Spending a solitary 2018 in Jackson WY, creative
energies rebloomed. He now performs original songs and covers on the acoustic guitar with musical roots
tracing back to American folksingers Bob Dylan and Jim Croce. Further, equal influence can be heard from
alternative rock and more contempory acts like Elliott Smith or the Avett Brothers.
